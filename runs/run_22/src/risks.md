# Potential Risks in Current Business Direction

## 1. Overreliance on Automation for Decision-Making
- **Description**: The Synthesis Engine's automation of daily operations could lead to overconfidence in automated insights, potentially neglecting the human intuition and expertise necessary for nuanced decision-making.
- **Mitigation Strategy**: Implement a dual-check system where key decisions are reviewed by senior management before final approval.

## 2. Rapid Market Expansion Without Adequate Research
- **Description**: Expanding into emerging markets via digital platforms (OPT-002) without thorough market research could result in wasted resources and ineffective marketing strategies.
- **Mitigation Strategy**: Conduct comprehensive market analysis to identify viable entry points and tailor strategies accordingly before full-scale deployment.

## 3. Inadequate Data Privacy Measures for Enhanced Analytics
- **Description**: Enhancing data analytics capabilities (OPT-003) without robust privacy measures could expose the company to legal risks and damage customer trust due to potential misuse or breaches of personal information.
- **Mitigation Strategy**: Ensure compliance with all relevant data protection regulations and implement strict internal policies for handling sensitive data.
